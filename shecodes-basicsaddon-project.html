Improv Comedy
at Chicago's Second City

The Second City is an improvisational comedy enterprise. It's the oldest improvisational theater troupe to be continuously based in Chicago and has become one of the most influential and prolific comedy theatres in the English-speaking world.

Improvisation is a form of theatre, often comedy, in which most or all of what is performed is unplanned and created spontaneously by the performers. In its purest form, the dialogue, action, story, and characters are created collaboratively by the players as the scene unfolds in present time, without use of an already prepared, written script.
